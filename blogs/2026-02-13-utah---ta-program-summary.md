Utah - TA Program Summary Tiffany.Hyatt@… Fri, 02/13/2026 - 09:16 SSBCI Technical Assistance (TA) Program Overview Utah has been approved for $1,249,137 in SSBCI Technical Assistance grant funding and implements the program to provide accounting, legal, and financial advisory support to small and underserved businesses. The TA program assists businesses with securing capital through loans or other funding sources. Total allocation: $1.2 million Program information updated in May 2025.
